Academic Calendar

The academic year is divided into Odd and Even semesters. Regular class tests, assignments, industrial visits, internships and project reviews are conducted throughout the year. Students are encouraged to participate in hackathons, technical symposiums and research activities.

Faculty Excellence

EIT has more than 150 highly qualified faculty members, including Ph.D holders, industry experts and researchers. Faculty members regularly publish research papers, attend international conferences and mentor students for innovation and entrepreneurship.
